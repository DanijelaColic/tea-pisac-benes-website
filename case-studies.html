Fashion E-commerce Platform: Complete Technical Overhaul

How a comprehensive technical SEO audit and implementation increased organic traffic by 150% and conversion rate by 45%

The Challenge

A mid-sized fashion e-commerce store was experiencing declining organic traffic despite having quality products and competitive pricing. The site had technical issues affecting both user experience and search engine crawling.

  • Page load times averaging 8+ seconds
  • Poor mobile experience with CLS issues
  • Duplicate content across product variations
  • Inefficient site architecture causing crawl budget waste
  • Missing or poorly implemented schema markup

The Solution

1

Technical Audit & Analysis

Comprehensive site crawl using Screaming Frog and Sitebulb to identify all technical issues. Core Web Vitals analysis and mobile usability assessment.

2

Performance Optimization

Image optimization, CSS/JS minification, CDN implementation, and server response time improvements. Reduced page load time from 8s to 2.1s.

3

Site Architecture Restructure

Reorganized category hierarchy, implemented proper internal linking strategy, and optimized URL structure for better crawlability.

4

Schema & Content Optimization

Implemented Product, Review, and Organization schema markup. Resolved duplicate content issues with canonical tags and parameter handling.

Electronics Store: Seamless Magento to Shopify Plus Migration

Zero traffic loss migration with 60% performance improvement and enhanced user experience

The Challenge

A growing electronics retailer needed to migrate from Magento 1.x to Shopify Plus due to end-of-life support and performance issues. The main concerns were preserving SEO equity and maintaining business continuity.

  • Complex product catalog with 50,000+ SKUs
  • Established SEO rankings for competitive keywords
  • Custom functionality and integrations
  • Tight timeline due to security concerns
  • Multiple international markets and currencies

The Solution

1

Pre-Migration SEO Audit

Complete inventory of all URLs, meta data, and ranking keywords. Identified critical pages and created comprehensive redirect mapping strategy.

2

URL Structure Planning

Designed new URL structure maintaining SEO-friendly patterns while leveraging Shopify's capabilities. Preserved category hierarchies and product URL patterns.

3

Content & Data Migration

Migrated all product data, meta information, and content while improving structure. Implemented enhanced schema markup and optimized product descriptions.

4

Testing & Launch

Extensive testing on staging environment, 301 redirect implementation, and careful monitoring during and after launch to ensure zero traffic loss.
